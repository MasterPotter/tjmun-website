TJMUN Summer Boot Camp Sign Up

Please fill out this form to RSVP for this year's TJMUN Summer Bootcamp. If you're an incoming TJ freshman interested in MUN, we highly encourage you to attend, regardless of your past experience! We'll have two days of getting accustomed to MUN at TJ and then a mock to wrap up the camp.

Note that participating in this camp is required for incoming freshmen in order to attend our first conference, McMUNC. Registration will be first come, first serve. We're excited to see all of you this August!
